Basic Details

  • Inoculation: Inoculation is the process of introducing Cordyceps militaris spores or, more commonly, liquid culture containing mycelium into a sterile substrate (such as rice, germinated soybeans, or a nutrient broth) to initiate growth.
  • Germination: Germination refers to the process where Cordyceps militaris spores or mycelium begin to colonize the new substrate, sprouting into hyphae that spread through it.
